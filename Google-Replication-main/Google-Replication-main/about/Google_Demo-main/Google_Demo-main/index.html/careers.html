Frequently Asked Questions

What is the interview process like?

We typically have a 4-step process: Intro Call, Technical Screen, Onsite (Virtual) Loop, and Final Offer. We aim to complete the entire cycle in 2 weeks.

Can I work 100% remotely?

Yes! We are a remote-first company. We have team members in 15+ countries and provide a stipend to set up your home office.

What equipment do you provide?

Every new employee gets a top-spec MacBook Pro (or equivalent), a 4K monitor, and noise-canceling headphones.

Do you sponsor visas?

Yes, for specific technical roles, we offer visa sponsorship and relocation assistance to our hub cities.
